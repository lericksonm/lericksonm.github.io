Resetting the Table

Re-Setting the Table is a restaurateur workshop concept that reimagines the role design has in small restaurants by introducing new owners to appropriate and applicable user centered design thinking methods. Based upon ethnographic research of successful and closed restaurants, and their owners, in Lawrence, Kansas, Re-Setting the Table is a workshop composed of design thinking tools for helping restaurant owners navigate and overcome the difficulties of getting started and finding success.

HSL City bikes

In 2019, HSL introduced city bikes in Vantaa. A completely new dockless city bike system meant that we had to rethink the whole site structure from scratch, as there now was two different bike systems under the same HSL brand.

The new website offers an effortless way to buy daily, weekly and season passes for both bike systems. Users can also view their personal details, trip history and update payment details from the site.
